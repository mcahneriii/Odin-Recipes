Savory Chicken

Looking for a mouth savory Chicken recipe? Well you've come to the right place, This chicken recipe is sure to be packed with lots of juice and a ton of flavor! Sure to fill up and appease any person looking for an amazing chicken recipe!

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon
  • 2 Tbsp of butter
  • Oregano

Creation of the chicken

  1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper, then top it off with Oregano.
  2. Create little silts in the top of the chicken to make sure it cooks evenly and thoroughly
  3. Set pan to medium heat
  4. Slice the 2 Tbsp of butter into two peices and let it dissolve on the pan.
  5. Throw the seasoned chicken in on top
  6. Grate some lemon skin on top of the chicken
  7. Baste butter on top of the chicken
  8. Flip and cook all sides of the chicken
  9. Cook until internal temperature reaches 125F
  10. Allow chicken to set for 5 minutes
  11. Serve your savory and juicy chicken.
