Savory Salmon

Looking for a mouth savory salmon recipe? Well you've come to the right place, This salmon recipe is sure to be packed with lots of juice and a ton of flavor! Sure to fill up and appease any person looking for an amazing salmon recipe!

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon
  • 2 Tbsp of butter
  • Oregano

Creation of the salmon

  1. Season the salmon with salt and pepper, then top it off with Oregano.
  2. Create little silts in the top of the salmon to make sure it cooks evenly and thoroughly
  3. Set pan to medium heat
  4. Slice the 2 Tbsp of butter into two peices and let it dissolve on the pan.
  5. Throw the seasoned salmon in on top
  6. Grate some lemon skin on top of the salmon
  7. Baste butter on top of the salmon
  8. Flip and cook all sides of the salmon
  9. Cook until internal temperature reaches 125F
  10. Allow salmon to set for 5 minutes
  11. Serve your savory and juicy salmon.
