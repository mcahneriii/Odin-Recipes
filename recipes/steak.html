Savory Steak

If you've always been a fan of juicy and savory steak you'll love this recipe! Marinated and tested to be delicious. You'll have plenty of juice and a delectable rich flavor that you'll want to make time and time again!

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon
  • 2 Tbsp of butter
  • Oregano

Creation of the Steak

  1. Season the steak with salt and pepper, then top it off with Oregano.
  2. Create little silts in the top of the steak to make sure it cooks evenly and thoroughly
  3. Set pan to medium heat
  4. Slice the 2 Tbsp of butter into two peices and let it dissolve on the pan.
  5. Throw the seasoned steak in on top
  6. Grate some lemon skin on top of the steak
  7. Baste butter on top of the steak
  8. Flip and cook all sides of the steak
  9. Cook until internal temperature reaches 125F
  10. Allow steak to set for 5 minutes
  11. Serve your savory and juicy steak.
